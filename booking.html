Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical booking lead time?

We recommend submitting booking requests 2-4 weeks in advance for local events and 4-8 weeks for international bookings. Rush bookings may be available for additional fees.

How are fees determined?

Artist fees depend on several factors: event size, location, travel distance, performance duration, equipment needs, and date availability. We provide custom quotes for each inquiry.

What's included in the booking?

Standard bookings include the artist(s), basic sound check, performance setup, and technical support. Additional services like full production, staging, or special equipment can be arranged for additional fees.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! We typically require a 50% deposit to secure the booking, with the remaining balance due one week before the event. Custom payment arrangements can be discussed.

Are cancellations refundable?

Cancellations more than 30 days before the event receive a 75% refund. Cancellations within 30 days may forfeit the deposit. Force majeure events are handled case-by-case.

Can we customize the setlist?

Absolutely! Artists work with event organizers to create the perfect setlist. Request your favorite songs and we'll do our best to accommodate.

What technical equipment do you need?

Specific technical requirements vary by artist. We provide a technical rider with each booking confirmation. Most artists need standard PA system, lighting, and stage setup.

Do you book for international events?

Yes! We book artists internationally. International bookings may include additional costs for travel, accommodations, and visas. We handle all logistics and coordination.
