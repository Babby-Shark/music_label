About Volt Records

Empowering independent artists since 2018

Our Story

From Vision to Reality

Volt Records was founded in 2018 by a collective of music industry veterans who saw a gap in the market. Traditional record labels often prioritized short-term profits over artist development. We wanted to create something different—a label that genuinely invested in its artists' success.

What started as a small operation with three founding members has grown into a powerhouse with a team of 50+ dedicated professionals across multiple offices worldwide.

Our Foundation

🎯 Mission

To discover, develop, and elevate exceptional musical talent by providing comprehensive artist services, cutting-edge production, and strategic marketing in the modern music landscape.

🚀 Vision

To become the most respected and artist-centric independent music label globally, known for nurturing creativity, driving commercial success, and building sustainable careers.

💎 Core Values
