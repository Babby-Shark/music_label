About Volt Records

Discover our mission

Founded in 2018, Volt Records is a forward-thinking independent music label dedicated to discovering, developing, and promoting exceptional talent across genres. We believe in the power of music to transform lives and communities.

Our team of music industry veterans brings decades of combined experience in artist management, production, distribution, and marketing. We're committed to providing our artists with comprehensive support at every stage of their career.

Learn More

Our Vision

To become the most artist-friendly independent music label, recognized for nurturing creativity and driving commercial success in the digital music era.

Our Values

Integrity, creativity, collaboration, and innovation guide everything we do. We prioritize artist wellbeing and long-term success over short-term gains.
