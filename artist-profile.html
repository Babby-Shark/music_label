Biography

Luna Echo emerged onto the music scene in 2019 with a distinctive blend of synthwave, electronic, and ambient music. With influences ranging from 80s synth-pop to modern electronic production, Luna has crafted a unique sonic identity that resonates with listeners worldwide.

Since joining Volt Records in 2021, Luna has released three critically acclaimed albums, each exploring different facets of electronic music production. Her latest album "Neon Dreams" reached #3 on the indie charts and garnered over 50 million streams.

Beyond music production, Luna is passionate about music education and frequently conducts workshops on synthesizer programming and music production techniques.
