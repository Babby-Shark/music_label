Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. During weekends and holidays, responses may take longer.

How do I become an artist with Volt Records?

Interested artists should contact our A&R department at artists@voltrecords.com with your music portfolio, bio, and press kit. We review all submissions and respond to promising candidates.

Can I distribute my music through Volt Records?

Yes! We offer distribution services for independent artists. Contact distribution@voltrecords.com or visit our services page to learn more about our distribution packages.

Do you accept business proposals and partnerships?

We're always interested in strategic partnerships and collaborations. Send your proposal to partnerships@voltrecords.com with relevant details about your business or service.

How do I book an artist from Volt Records?

Visit our booking page or email booking@voltrecords.com with your event details. Our team will work with you to find the perfect artist for your needs.

What are your investment opportunities?

We offer equity investments, revenue sharing, and debt financing options. Contact our investor relations team at investors@voltrecords.com for detailed information.
